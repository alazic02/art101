About:

I am a digital and traditional artist who specializes in illustration, as well as a linguist and game developer. My native language is English; I am a heritage speaker of Spanish, and am at an intermediate level in Japanese. Other languages include Java, JavaScript, and HTML.

I have a double B.A. from UCSC in Art&Design, Games and Playable Media (AGPM) and Applied Linguistics (APLX) with a focus in Japanese. These two programs are a culmination of my main interests: how interactive art, narrative, and language impacts every day life and culture.
